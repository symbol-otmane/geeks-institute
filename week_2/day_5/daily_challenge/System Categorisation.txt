Zelda (Zelda syndrome)
Simple system : Break Jars - chance to get items

Dofus (Crafting system)
Complicated system : Gather resources from natural source & monster - craft items - random values on craft

Warframe (Crafting system)
Complex system : Do missions - missions have rotations AABC - different rewards in each rotation - among rewards are relics - relics have 6 rewards you only get 1 out of 6 with different % - the rewards are components to craft or to use for a craft
